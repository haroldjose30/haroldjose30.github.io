RDS Web

Asp.net Core

Overview

The RDS(Reception and Dispatch System) is a Webapp for receiving and shipping grain in ports and warehouses. This system controls the entry, loading, quality analysis, unloading and exit of trucks at the port and warehouse. It was developed in asp.net core mvc on the frontend, and asp.net core webapi on the backend.


Responsabilities

As Fullstack .net developer my responsibilities were to perform maintenance on the frontend, backend,create new features, and improvements on integrations with Plc devices, serial port devices and RFID Devices.
